About Lol Pick'em!

Welcome to my Lol Pick'em app! This is meant to be a light-hearted, quick and easy game to play without a huge time commitment but that still gives people opportunity to think about League of Legends, strategy, meta, etc. The game is quite simple: you are presented with a real Lol game's team composition and you choose which team you think won. To check it out, head over to the play page and click the "play" button

Game Facts:

  • All game information is provided by the Riot API
  • You must login, all your game history will be saved to your user profile! Later, I will make your history available to you

Why do you have to make an account?

  • This is a free app, using a developer API key from Riot, thus I need to be able to restrict traffic from the open internet
  • You can either create an account locally with my app and Auth0 or you can sign in with Google
  • In any case, I will not use, distribute or exploit your personal information for any reason

Why am I making this?

  • A quick and easy way to engage with LoL
  • I hadn't seen this type of fantasy game for LoL
  • Thinking about team composition is fun and engaging for me

In the future, I would like to add:

  • A page for a user to view their history
  • Hints for which team got first blood or first tower, hot streaks, dragon count, KDA, game duration, etc
  • The ability to search for a summoner by name
  • Sharing game information with other users
  • Implementing milestones & challenges
